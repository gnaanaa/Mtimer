# Privacy Policy for MTimer

**Effective Date:** April 22, 2026

MTimer ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application ("the app"). By using MTimer, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 1. Overview

MTimer is a minimalist meditation timer. We prioritize privacy by design. All data created in the app is stored locally on your device unless you explicitly choose to enable optional cloud features.

We do not operate any servers. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers of our own.

---

## 2. Health Connect Integration

MTimer integrates with **Android Health Connect** to allow you to sync your meditation sessions and physiological data with other health and fitness apps on your device.

**Data accessed:**
- *Write:* Mindfulness Session records (session start time, end time, duration, and title)
- *Read:* Mindfulness Session records (to calculate and display your weekly meditation total)
- *Read:* Heart Rate records (to display physiological feedback during and after sessions)

**How we use this data:**
- We write completed meditation sessions to Health Connect so other compatible apps can include them in your activity summary.
- We read Mindfulness Session records solely to display your weekly meditation minutes within MTimer.
- We read Heart Rate records to help you understand your body's relaxation response during meditation.

**Data sharing:**
- We do not share, sell, transfer, or disclose your Health Connect data to any third party, advertiser, or external server.
- Health Connect data is transferred only between MTimer and the Health Connect data store on your device.

**Limited Use:**
Our use of data obtained through Health Connect is limited to providing and improving the features of MTimer. We do not use Health Connect data for advertising, and we do not allow any person to read this data unless necessary to provide the in-app feature. Our practices comply with the [Health Connect Permissions policy](https://developer.android.com/health-and-fitness/guides/health-connect/publish/policy), including its Limited Use requirements.

---

## 3. Google Drive Cloud Backup (Optional)

If you choose to enable Cloud Backup, MTimer uses your **Google Drive App Data folder** to store backups of your meditation presets.

**Scope of access:**
- We access only the hidden App Data folder assigned to MTimer by Google Drive
- We cannot see, read, or modify any other files in your Google Drive

**Purpose:**
- To allow you to restore your presets if you change devices or reinstall the app

**How to revoke:**
- You can disconnect MTimer from Google Drive at any time via Google Account settings → Security → Third-party apps with account access

---

## 4. Local Data Storage

All meditation sessions, presets, and app settings are stored in a local database on your device. This data:
- Never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable Cloud Backup
- Can be deleted at any time by clearing the app's data in Android Settings

---

## 5. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:
- Your name, email address, or any account information (unless you sign in for Cloud Backup, in which case only your Google account identifier is used locally to authenticate Drive access)
- Location data
- Device identifiers for advertising
- Usage analytics or crash reports sent to our servers
- Any data from children

## 7. Children's Privacy

Because MTimer does not collect any personal information from any user, it is safe for use by individuals of all ages. We do not knowingly collect, store, or share any personal data from children under the age of 13.

---

## 8. Data Security

All data stored locally on your device is protected by Android's application sandboxing. Cloud backups stored in Google Drive App Data are protected by your Google account credentials and Google's own security infrastructure. We do not transmit your data over any network of our own.

---

## 9. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

1. Delete individual sessions or presets within the app
2. Clear all app data via **Android Settings → Apps → MTimer → Storage → Clear Data**
3. Remove cloud backups by disconnecting MTimer from your Google Account or by uninstalling the app (Drive App Data is deleted when you remove app access)
