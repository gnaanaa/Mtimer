# Privacy Policy for MTimer

**Effective Date:** October 25, 2024

MTimer ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Overview
MTimer is a minimalist meditation timer. We prioritize user privacy by design. Most data created in the app is stored locally on your device.

## 2. Health Connect Data
MTimer integrates with **Google Health Connect** to allow you to sync your meditation sessions with other health and fitness apps.
*   **Data Accessed:** We read and write "Mindfulness Session" and "Heart Rate" records.
*   **How we use it:** We write your completed meditation sessions to Health Connect so other apps can count them toward your daily activity. We read heart rate data only to display it to you during or after a session (if enabled).
*   **Data Sharing:** We do not share your Health Connect data with any third-party servers or advertisers. It is transferred only between MTimer and the Health Connect storage on your device.
*   **Policy Compliance:** Our use of information received from Health Connect will adhere to the Health Connect Permissions policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

## 3. Google Drive Data (Cloud Backup)
If you enable Cloud Backup, MTimer uses your **Google Drive App Data folder** to store backups of your meditation presets and history.
*   **Access Scope:** We only access our own "hidden" App Data folder. We cannot see, read, or modify any other files in your Google Drive.
*   **Purpose:** To allow you to restore your meditation history if you change devices.

## 4. Local Data Storage
All meditation presets and history records are stored in a local SQLite database on your device. This data remains on your device unless you explicitly use the Cloud Backup or Export features.

## 5. Third-Party Services
*   **Google Play Services:** Used for app updates and basic functionality.
*   **Google Sign-In:** Used to authenticate your Google Drive for backups.

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion
You can delete all your data at any time by:
1. Deleting individual history records or presets within the app.
2. Clearing the app's cache/data in Android Settings.
3. Disconnecting MTimer from your Google Account settings to remove cloud backups.
